"I dream of painting and then I paint my dream" - Vincent Van Gogh

The first lesson I attended was when I was 10 years old and went with my friend to his lesson. The artist who was teaching hime gave me a lot of attention and helped draw my first pastel drawing. It was a pear leaning on a vase and it is still hung on the wall in my parents appartment. With a little bit of his help, the outcome turned out to be really good and he suggested that I should start taking lessons.

Since that day, I started taking lessons weekly for over 6 years and due to my improvements, I started drawing oil paintings too. Eventually, the lessons became more of a time for me to draw peacfully in a quiet place, and all the teacher did was correct me sometimes and/or give me his personal opinion. Ultimetely, I stopped taking classes but I kept dawing in my free time. Till this day, my parent's appartment is still full of my paintings. You can find them on every wall of every room.
